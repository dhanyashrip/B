Decentralized Supply Chain Management Using Blockchain

Ensuring transparency, traceability, and security across global supply networks with blockchain technology.

About the System

A decentralized supply chain leverages blockchain to create a tamper-proof ledger of transactions. Each participant in the supply chain can verify and record data securely, eliminating fraud and increasing efficiency.

Key Features

Transparency

All transactions are visible and verifiable by stakeholders.

Traceability

Track products from origin to delivery in real-time.

Security

Cryptographic validation ensures data integrity.

Smart Contracts

Automated agreements reduce manual intervention and errors.

How It Works

Step 1: Product Registration

Manufacturer registers product details on blockchain.

Step 2: Distribution

Each transaction is recorded and verified.

Step 3: Retail & Consumer Access

Consumers verify authenticity via blockchain records.
